About Us

Welcome to Park Physical Therapy Associates! We have been providing our neighbors here in our community with quality care since 1977. As an organization with an established history of helping those we meet, we understand how important living a healthy life is to our clients. We consider all those who enter through our doors to be family, and we do our best to make sure that they recieve the best treatment available to help alleviate any affliction they may be suffering from. Feel free to stop by and explore one or both of our two facilities! We will be more than happy to welcome you to our Park PT Family!

Our Services

Hands On Manual Therapy

Each of our facilities contain individual treatment areas designed to protect our patient's privacy and promote a healing enviroment. Each treatment includes therapeutic methods such as Soft Tissue Manipulation, Joint Mobilization, and Passive Range of Motion. Our therapists also are trained and specializing in the use of modalities such as Thermotherapy, Cryotherapy, Hydrotherapy, Electric stimulation, and Ultrasound.

Routine Strength and Endurance Training

After being evaluated, one of our therapists will craft you an exercise program to help strengthen, stabilize, and revitalize the affected area so that your body can start on it's path to healing. And as you progress, a home exercise sheet will be provided to help promote a healthy routine while at home.

Balance Training and Fall Prevention

After going through certain surgical procedures, physical or neurological pathologies, or even just the course of getting older, we may see a dramatic change in our stability and balance. Our therapists will work alongside you to improve core and lower extremity stabilization, stair negotiation, and even the use of assistive devices.

And More

We are trained to treat a large variety of conditions. We strive for excellence, and are constantly researching new ways to help those we meet with their afflictions. Both of our clinics utilize multiple approaches to help manage your symptoms. Our main objective is to return you to a state free of pain and limitations.
